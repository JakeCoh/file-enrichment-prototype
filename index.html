Input Fields

Email Address

The email address for any person.

Example: jane.smith@gmail.com

Phone Number

The phone number for any person.

Example: +1-303-555-5555

Social Profile URL

URLs, IDs, and usernames for social profiles.

Example: linkedin.com/in/jane-smith

Name & Address

The first and last name for any person combined with their street address, city, state and/or country.

Example: Jane Smith, 1234 Main Street Denver, Colorado

Mobile Advertising ID (MAID)

Connect the offline and online worlds and improve targeted advertising by appending Mobile Advertising IDs to your current contact information.

Example: 4LIHAH9T-BY0W-UF42-OGWM-FI29NEYAFW7TH
